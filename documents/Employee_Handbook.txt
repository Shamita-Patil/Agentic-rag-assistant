Employee Handbook

Working Hours
Employees are expected to work from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Vacation Policy
Employees are entitled to 20 paid vacation days per year.

Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 paid sick leave days annually.

Code of Conduct
Employees must maintain professional behavior and comply with company policies.
